A quick look at our most popular adventures

Lion’s Head Summit

The ultimate Cape Town adventure! This roughly 3.5 hour, moderate hike offers breathtaking 360-degree views of Table Mountain, the Atlantic Ocean, and the city skyline. Perfect for sunset or sunrise to experience the magic of golden hour lighting.

Explore one of the new 7 wonders of nature! Choose from thrilling routes like India Venster or the classic Platteklip Gorge, tailored to your fitness level, with safe, knowledgeable guides leading the way.
